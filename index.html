About Technolane

At Technolane, we transform ideas into engineered reality. Our expertise spans from concept design to certified engineering models using AutoCAD, SolidWorks, STAAD, and Ansys. We deliver high-quality CNC machining, fabrication, and engineering solutions for industries including aerospace, defense, medical, and semiconductor.

Our values: Precision, Quality, Safety, and Innovation.

Our Services

Engineering Design & Certification

From sketches to certified engineering models using AutoCAD, SolidWorks, STAAD, and Ansys. We ensure precise and dependable designs tailored to your needs.

CNC Swiss Screw Machining

Precision-crafted components with tight tolerances and fine finishes. Capabilities include CITIZEN A20 and IMCA Bar feeder, handling part diameters up to 25mm across various metals and plastics.

Fabrication

In partnership with FerroCraft, we provide heavy structural fabrication and I-beam work. Our joint expertise delivers superior results for industrial projects.

Technical Services & Spare Parts

We offer service contracts, value-added solutions, and spare parts support to keep your operations running smoothly.
